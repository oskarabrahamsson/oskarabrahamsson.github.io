Oskar Abrahamsson

About

I am a PhD student in the Formal Methods division at Chalmers University of Technology. My supervisor is Magnus Myreen.

Research

My research is about theorem provers and software verification. I am interested in the use of interactive theorem provers as tools for software development. Most of my work is done using HOL4. I am an active contributor to the CakeML project.

Teaching

I am (or have been) a teaching assistant in the following courses at Chalmers/GU:

  • TIN093/DIT600 Algorithms
  • TDA294/DIT271 Formal Methods in Software Development
  • TDA283/DIT300 Compiler Construction
  • DAT026/DIT991 Mathematical Modelling and Problem Solving
